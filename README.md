# Retail Sales Intelligence & Forecasting Platform

## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end retail sales intelligence solution developed using pizza sales data. It combines SQL-based analysis, interactive Power BI dashboards, and Python time-series forecasting to analyze revenue trends, order patterns, category performance, and product-level sales insights.

## Tools & Technologies

- SQL Server
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Statsmodels
- Business Intelligence

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Total Pizzas Sold
- Daily and Monthly Sales Trends
- Revenue Contribution by Pizza Category and Size

### 2. Best & Worst Sellers

- Top and Bottom Pizzas by Revenue
- Top and Bottom Pizzas by Quantity Sold
- Top and Bottom Pizzas by Total Orders

### 3. Forecast & Executive Intelligence

- Monthly Revenue Forecast
- Monthly Order Forecast
- Executive Business Insights
- Actionable Business Recommendations

## Key Insights

- Identified peak sales periods and monthly revenue trends.
- Evaluated the best- and worst-performing pizzas.
- Analyzed revenue contribution across pizza categories and sizes.
- Forecasted future revenue and order demand using Python.

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize high-performing pizzas in promotional campaigns.
- Introduce combo offers for low-performing products.
- Optimize staffing and inventory based on expected demand.
- Monitor forecast trends to support sales planning.

## Project Outcome

The platform transforms raw pizza sales data into interactive business intelligence dashboards, predictive forecasts, and actionable recommendations to support data-driven retail decision-making.
